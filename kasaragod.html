Kasaragod is a municipal town and the district headquarters of Kasaragod district of Kerala state in India. Situated in the rich biodiversity of Western Ghats, it is known for the Chandragiri and Bekal Forts, Chandragiri River, historic Kolathiri Rajas, natural environment of Ranipuram and Kottancheri Hills, historical and religious sites like the Madiyan Kulom temple, Madhur Temple, Ananthapuram Lake Temple and Malik Deenar Mosque. It is located 50 km south of the major port city & a commercial hub Mangalore and 578 km north of state capital Thiruvananthapuram.The major political parties are CPI(M), CPI, Indian Union Muslim League, INC and BJP. North Kasaragod is dominated by BJP and IUML, and the south is dominated by CPI(M)

Bekal Fort

Bekal Fort was built by Shivappa Nayaka of Keladi in 1650 AD, at Bekal. It is the largest fort in Kerala, spreading over 40 acres (160,000 m2).The fort appears to emerge from the sea. Almost three-quarters of its exterior is in contact with water. Bekal fort was not an administrative centre and does not include any palaces or mansions. An important feature is the water-tank, magazine and the flight of steps leading to an observation tower built by Tipu Sultan.

The Mallam Durga Parameshwari Temple is located in Muliyar Village near Bovikanam, in Kasargod District of Kerala. The mallam durga parameshwari temple is Centuries old. The presiding deity of this temple is Goddess Durga Parameshwari. This temple constructed by the karate brahmins of maharashtra, is well maintained.

Ranipuram Peak

The Ranipuram Peak stands 750 metres above the sea level in the hill station of Vellarikundu Taluk of Kerala. Lovingly called the “Ooty of Kerala”, the hill is a popular honeymoon spot, thanks to its verdant mountains of the KottencheriTalakaveri Range. With shoals, grasslands and evergreen monsoon forestry, the Ranipuram Peak forms a part of the Western Ghat protected area. It stands as a merger peak between the Ranipuram Wildlife Sanctuary and the Talakaveri Wildlife Sanctuary, thereby is rich in biodiversity.
